Our App Matrix

Six carefully crafted offline tool categories

From mountain trails to family receipts, our portfolio on Google Play supports the rituals that matter — and keeps every detail stored securely on your own device.

Outdoor Offline Route Parser

Decode, replay and export GPX, KML and FIT files anywhere — even with no signal. Designed for explorers, search-and-rescue teams and trail runners.

Collectibles Asset Archive

Catalog coins, cards, sneakers, watches and any treasured collection with valuation timelines, photo dossiers and tamper-proof local records.

Textual Inspiration Engine

A writers' sanctuary for fragments, prompts and unfinished verses. Searchable, taggable and completely offline — your drafts never leave your phone.

Exercise Load Calculator

Estimate training load, recovery windows and weekly TSS without sharing biometric data. Personalised, science-backed and synced only on your terms.

Family Receipt Scanner

Capture, OCR and categorise household receipts, warranties and invoices locally. Built for tax season and household budgeting without cloud uploads.

Periodic Habit Tracker

Design cycles, streaks and rituals on your own terms. A calm, ad-light habit journal for routines that grow stronger with time — not notifications.

Mobile Management Application

A single companion for your entire Jingrui toolset

The Jingrui Console mobile management app is the central nervous system of our product family. From one beautifully designed dashboard you can install, license, update, mirror, back-up and remotely audit every Jingrui Xinyuan app installed on your device — and on the devices of family members you trust.

Built for power users and prosumers across Europe and North America, the console gives you the visibility and the controls of an enterprise MDM, while staying gentle enough for everyday home use.

  • One-tap install, license and update for every Jingrui app
  • Local-only sync of usage stats — nothing leaves your device
  • Granular family sharing with role-based controls
  • Encrypted local backups, fully exportable in open formats
