Our Apps & Mobile Management Platform

Six carefully crafted offline tools for travel, collectibles, creativity, fitness, family and personal growth — and the Jingrui Console that ties them together into one calm, private, ad-light mobile management platform.

App Matrix

Six offline-first app categories, all on Google Play

Each app is engineered around a "data never leaves the device" philosophy, while shipping with the modern monetisation and compliance features that Google Play demands in every region.

Outdoor Offline Route Parser

Decode GPX, KML, FIT and TCX files fully offline. Render elevation, splits and 3D previews. Replay, edit and export your adventures anywhere — even on the summit.

  • • Offline map tiles with country packs
  • • GPX/KML/FIT/TCX round-trip support
  • • Barometric + GPS fusion for accuracy

Collectibles Asset Archive

A private museum for coins, sneakers, watches, cards, vinyl and anything else you collect. Valuation timelines, photo dossiers, condition notes — all stored locally with end-to-end encrypted export.

  • • 200+ category templates
  • • Encrypted JSON / CSV export
  • • Loss-prevention & insurance reports

Textual Inspiration Engine

A writers' sanctuary for fragments, prompts, outlines, and verses. Powerful tagging, full-text search and lightning-fast keyboard make it a delight for daily practice.

  • • Markdown + rich-text composer
  • • Local semantic search
  • • Export to ePub, PDF, DOCX

Exercise Load Calculator

Compute TSS, CTL, ATL and TSB for any training plan, on any watch. Personalised recovery windows, weekly summaries and a beautifully simple dashboard — no cloud sync required.

  • • 30+ sport profiles
  • • Heart-rate, pace & power zones
  • • Wear OS & iOS companion sync (local)

Family Receipt Scanner

On-device OCR for receipts, warranties and invoices. Smart categorisation, multi-currency totals, and tax-ready export — perfect for the household budget without ever touching the cloud.

  • • 100% on-device OCR
  • • Multi-currency & tax tagging
  • • Family share via local network

Periodic Habit Tracker

Design cycles, streaks and rituals on your own terms. A calm, ad-light habit journal with rich analytics — built for routines that grow stronger with time, not notifications.

  • • Custom cadence (daily, weekly, lunar)
  • • Heatmaps & streak analytics
  • • Encrypted journal entries

Monetisation & Compliance

Built for Google Play's full monetisation stack

Our apps support both IAA (In-App Advertising) and IAP (In-App Purchases) models — and are integrated with the leading first-tier ad networks, mediation platforms and payment providers so that users in any region can transact in their preferred way.

IAA — In-App Advertising

Honest, opt-in ad placements powered by the world's leading first-tier ad networks, mediation platforms and bidding solutions — all privacy-compliant.

  • AdMob / Google Ads (splash, banner, interstitial, rewarded)
  • Meta Audience Network (banner, interstitial, rewarded, native)
  • Unity Ads & Unity LevelPlay mediation
  • AppLovin MAX mediation & bidding
  • Pangle (TikTok) for global reach
  • ironSource (Unity) rewarded video
  • Vungle (Liftoff) rewarded & interstitial
  • Chartboost / InMobi programmatic demand
  • Mintegral / Tapjoy offerwall & rewarded
  • Digital Turbine / Fyber mediation fallback
  • Start.io / Smaato open-exchange bidding
  • Yandex Ads for CIS-region installs

IAP — In-App Purchases

Subscriptions, one-time unlocks and consumables via Google Play Billing — with regional pricing, family-library support and transparent receipts.

  • Google Play Billing v6 — subscriptions & one-time
  • Family Library sharing on supported plans
  • Grace period & account hold support
  • Regional price experimentation
  • Offer codes & promotional pricing
  • Real-time developer notifications
  • External content rules (EU compliance)
  • Purchase verification on-device (offline-capable)
  • Restore on new device via account
  • Tax-compliant receipts in 190+ countries
