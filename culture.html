Our Culture

We are a small, deliberate team of designers, engineers and writers who believe that software should respect the people who use it — and the people who don't, yet.

Our Mission

Make software that quietly does the right thing

Our mission is to build offline-first mobile tools that help people live richer, more deliberate lives — without ever asking them to give up ownership of their data. We design for the long-term, in software the way a good pair of hiking boots is designed: useful on the first trip and better on the hundredth.

Every Jingrui Xinyuan app is shipped with the same four commitments: privacy by default, beauty by intention, performance by craft, and accessibility by empathy.

Core Values

Five principles, applied everywhere

The values we use to make every product, hiring and partnership decision at Jingrui Xinyuan.

01

Privacy by Default

If a feature doesn't need the cloud to work, it doesn't talk to the cloud. Personal data belongs to the user — full stop.

02

Craft over Hype

We choose tools and techniques that have proven themselves. We ship fewer features, better. We sweat the small things.

03

Honest Monetisation

Our ads are clearly ads. Our IAP unlocks are clearly unlocks. We never sell data, and we never will.

04

Global & Local

We design for the strictest regional policies on Earth, so users everywhere get the same calm, respectful experience.

05

Long-Term Stewardship

Every Jingrui Xinyuan app is built to be maintained for a decade. We pick partners who share that horizon.

06

Accessible by Default

Screen readers, large text, high contrast, reduced motion — accessibility is a build-time decision, not an afterthought.

07

Calm Software

Notifications are opt-in. Dark patterns are forbidden. The product should never feel like it is shouting at you.

08

Joy in the Details

Great software is the sum of a thousand tiny delights. We give ourselves permission to obsess over each one.

Our Journey

From a garage in Miyun to 180 countries

A short timeline of the milestones that have shaped Jingrui Xinyuan.

2018

Founded in Beijing

Beijing Jingrui Xinyuan Technology Co., Ltd is incorporated in Miyun District, Beijing, with a focus on offline-first consumer mobile software.

2019

First App on Google Play

The Outdoor Offline Route Parser debuts on Google Play, gaining a devoted following among trail runners, cyclists and search-and-rescue teams.

2020

Crossed 1M Installs

Our portfolio reached one million lifetime installs across the first three apps, with users in 80+ countries.

2022

IAA + IAP Launched

We integrated the full Google Play monetisation stack — IAA with mediation, IAP with subscriptions and family library support.

2023

Jingrui Console (Mobile Management)

Our mobile management application launches, giving users a single dashboard to install, license and update every Jingrui Xinyuan app.

2024

Global Compliance Milestone

We achieved compliance certification with GDPR, CCPA, COPPA, LGPD and PIPL frameworks for our entire app portfolio.

2026

Serving 180+ Countries

Today, Jingrui Xinyuan apps are installed in 180+ countries, with 5M+ lifetime installs and an NPS of 67 — without ever collecting personal data.
